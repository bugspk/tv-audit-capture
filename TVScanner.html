Serial Scanner v0.7

How to use (tap to expand)
  1. Tap Scan to detect the TV barcode (or type serial manually). The scan button is next to the Serial field.
  2. Tap Take Photo to capture the TV picture.
  3. Tap Save as JPG to download it as <serial>.jpg (auto-downscaled to avoid memory errors).
  4. No page reload needed — memory is freed each time so you can keep going.
  5. Upload saved photos to your Google Drive YYYY_MM folder later.
