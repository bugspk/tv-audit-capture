Serial Scanner v0.93

How to use (tap to expand)
  1. Tap Scan from Photo → native camera opens → capture the barcode image (autofocus works in native camera).
  2. The image is decoded locally and fills the Serial field.
  3. Take the Audit Photo (the actual TV picture), verify filename/thumbnail, then tap Save as JPG to download <serial>.jpg.
  4. Upload saved JPGs to your Google Drive YYYY_MM folder later, then run your Sheet’s Insert/Update.
